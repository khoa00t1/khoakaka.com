## How to request deletion

1. Send an email to [support@khoakaka.com](mailto:support@khoakaka.com) from the
    address linked to your account.
2. Use the subject line: **"Delete my Clan Tree account"**.
3. We will verify your identity and confirm deletion within 30 days.

## What is deleted

* Your Firebase Authentication account
* Your user profile (email, display name, avatar)
* Your clan memberships and roles
* Subscription entitlement data linked to your account in our systems

## What may be kept

* Genealogy data you added to a shared clan may remain visible to other clan
    members until a clan admin removes it or deletes the clan.
* Purchase records may be retained by Google Play / RevenueCat as required for
    tax and fraud prevention (we do not control those retention periods).

## Retention

* We process deletion requests within 30 days.
* Backup copies may take up to 90 days to be fully purged from Firebase backups.
