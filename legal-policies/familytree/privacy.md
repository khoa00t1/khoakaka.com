Building great apps starts with trust. This page explains, in plain language,
what **Clan Tree** collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

In this policy, "we", "us" and "our" mean **Khoakaka**, the maker of Clan
Tree on Android, iOS and the Web.

## What we collect

* **Account information.** When you sign in with Google or with email and
    password, we receive your user ID, email address, display name and
    profile picture.
* **Your clan content.** Anything you create in the app — members,
    relationships, photos, notes, CSV imports — is saved on your behalf so
    you and your invited relatives can see and edit it across devices.
* **Subscription status.** If you buy a premium plan, we receive whether
    your subscription is active and which platform issued it. We never see
    your payment card details.
* **Diagnostics.** Crash reports and basic technical logs help us keep the
    app stable.

## How we use it

We use this information only to:

* Run, secure and improve Clan Tree.
* Sign you in and protect your account.
* Sync your clan across your devices and the relatives you invite.
* Answer your support requests.
* Comply with the law.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do not use your clan
content to train AI models or to show you ads.

## Who can see your content

Your clan content is visible only to the people you invite, and only at the
permission level you grant them (admin, editor or viewer).

We share data with a small number of trusted service providers that help us
run the app:

* **Google Firebase** — sign-in, database (Cloud Firestore) and photo
    storage (Cloud Storage).
* **Google Play and Apple App Store** — handle your subscription billing.
* **Crash reporting tools** — anonymous diagnostics only.

We may also share data if the law requires it, or to protect users from
serious harm.

## Where your data lives

Your data is processed on Google Cloud infrastructure used by Firebase. That
may include locations outside your country. The providers we use maintain
appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

## Your data, your choice

You can:

* **See and edit** your account and clan information directly in the app.
* **Export** your members as a CSV at any time.
* **Delete** your account, which removes the clans you own and your
    personal data within a reasonable period.

If you'd rather contact us to exercise any of these rights, see
[Contact us](#contact-us) below or our
[account deletion page]({{ '/legal-policies/familytree/delete-account.html' | relative_url }}).

## Children

Clan Tree is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in
your country). We don't knowingly collect personal information from
children. If you believe a child has shared information with us, contact us
and we will remove it.

## Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit and at
rest through Firebase. No system is 100% secure, but we work hard to protect
your data and to fix issues quickly.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the
top of this page and, when appropriate, notify you in the app.
